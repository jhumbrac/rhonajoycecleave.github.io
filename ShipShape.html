Scottish Enterprise Recruitment Campaign

The Future is Ship Shape was a campaign for Scottish Enterprise to recruit apprentices into the ship building industry. Aimed at school leavers and the unemployed, the campaign was highly publicised and very successful - all of the initial apprenticeship roles were filled within the first few days.

I commissioned a paper engineer to make the structure which was then photographed and used throughout the marketing material.

Art Director - Rhona Cleave, Copywriter - Richard Harris.
