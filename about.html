About Rhona


Rhona Cleave is an award winning graphic designer + art director, originally from Scotland but now living in the beautiful Waikato, New Zealand.

She attained her degree in graphic design at Dundee University + has almost 10 years experience in the creative industry, ranging from direct marketing + advertising to publication design + branding.

Rhona runs and studio, a boutique graphic design agency. And studio was founded with a desire to offer a range of professional design services to clients who are open to original thinking + strong ideas + have a desire to work with an experienced, passionate designer on their projects.
