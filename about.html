About Rhona

Rhona Cleave is an award winning graphic designer and art director, originally from Scotland but now living in the beautiful Waikato, New Zealand.

She attained her degree in graphic design at Dundee University and has almost 10 years experience in the creative industry, ranging from direct marketing and advertising to publication design and branding.

Rhona runs and studio, a boutique graphic design agency. and studio was founded with a desire to offer a range of professional design services to clients who are open to original thinking and strong ideas.
